FAQ

1. What services done SDXVISION provide?

We specialize in full-stack development: web and mobile applications, custom software solutions, SaaS platforms, Al agents, and model-training pipelines. We also deliver startup MVPs, urgent-turnaround projects, and B2B outsourcing for agencies and enterprises.

2. Can you build an MVP on a tight budget?

Absolutely. We work closely with you to understand core requirements and identify cost-effective tech stacks. By focusing on essential features first, we help early-stage founders launch viable MVPs without breaking the bank.

3. How do you guarantee project deadlines?

Every engagement begins with a clear scope, milestones, and delivery dates. We follow a transparent agile process-weekly check-ins, demos, and progress reports-so you always know where your project stands and can plan accordingly.

4. What is your B2B outsourcing model?

If you're an agency or company with overflow work, we act as your dedicated white-label development partner. You assign us projects at a competitive, fixed cost, and we deliver complete, production-ready code under your brand, with time guarantees on every milestone.

5. How quickly can you handle urgent projects?

For high-priority or fast-track assignments, we allocate focused sprint teams and compress our roadmap. Depending on complexity, we can stand up core functionality in as little as 24hours-then iterate rapidly to meet your deadline.

6. Which technologies do you work with?

Our core expertise includes React, Next.js, React Native,Flutter, Node.js, Python/Django, PHP/WordPress, and leading Al/ML frameworks. We select the most feasible technology for your needs to balance performance, cost, and maintainability.

7. How do you manage project budgets?

We conduct a detailed discovery and research phase to scope your project, then propose a fixed-cost estimate. Throughout development, we optimize workflows and leverage open-source tools or automation to keep expenses under control without sacrificing quality.

8. Do you offer post-launch support and maintenance?

Yes. After deployment, we provide optional support packages-bug fixes, performance tuning, and feature enhancements-to ensure your product stays secure, up-to-date, and aligned with evolving business needs.

9. How can I get started with SDX?

Simply reach out via our contact form or email us at [email protected] We'll schedule a free 30-minute discovery call to discuss your vision, challenges, and budget. From there, we'll draft a tailored proposal and project plan.

10. Do you sign NDAs and formal agreements?

Absolutely. We use standard Master Service Agreements (MSA) and project-specific Statements of Work (SOW) to outline scope, deliverables, IP rights, confidentiality, and payment terms-giving you full peace of mind.
